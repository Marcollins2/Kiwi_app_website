Interactive and user-friendly UI's


The Kiwi app boasts a user interface that is both intuitive and visually appealing, ensuring a seamless and enjoyable experience for users of all levels. Our design philosophy revolves around simplicity, functionality, and aesthetics, ensuring that you can effortlessly navigate the app and make the most of its features. Let's delve into the key aspects of our user interfaces:

User Registration and Login:

The user registration and login screen are the entry points to the app. New users can sign up by providing essential details such as their name, email, and password. Existing users can seamlessly log in using their credentials. This screen ensures a secure and personalized experience for each user, enabling them to access their personalized profile, saved items, and order history.

User Profile Management:

The user profile management screen allows users to personalize their experience within the app. Users can input their preferences for styles and colors. This screen empowers users to curate their fashion journey according to their tastes and needs, making the app feel tailored to their individual preferences.

Camera Interface for Body Scanning:

The camera interface screen offers an innovative feature that sets the Kiwi app apart. Users can utilize their device's camera to scan their body height and color. This information is used to provide more accurate fashion recommendations and outfit suggestions that cater to the user's unique physique and complexion. The interface ensures a user-friendly experience by guiding users through the scanning process step by step.

Fashion Store:

The fashion store screen is where users can immerse themselves in an extensive collection of clothing and accessories. The store is organized into categories, making it easy for users to browse through different styles, occasions, and trends. Each item is presented with high-quality images, detailed descriptions, prices, and availability.

These four screens together provide a comprehensive and user-centric experience within the Kiwi app. From registering and managing profiles to accessing a camera interface for personalized body scanning and exploring a diverse fashion store, every screen is designed to cater to the user's fashion journey and enhance their shopping experience. Whether you're starting your style exploration, seeking personalized recommendations, or shopping for the latest trends, the Kiwi app's screens are tailored to meet your needs and provide a seamless, engaging experience.
